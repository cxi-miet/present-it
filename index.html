Instructions

Instructions for Competition

  • ✔ Candidates can participate as Individual or as a Team (2 students only).
  • ✔ Participants are required to develop a presentation for a full fledged project idea on the given topic
  • ✔ Selection will be done on the basis of submitted project presentation
  • ✔ Project presentation must include the technical content like idea/approach of the project, technological stack, its features or objectives etc.
  • ✔ Top 10 selected participants/teams will be explaining or presenting their project ppt in front of the judges.
  • ✔ Cross questioning will take place: Judges will ask questions regarding to your project content.
  • ✔ Judging will be based on (70%) confidance & accuracy int the ppt explaination and (30%) the technical content and project ppt.

Rules

Rules for Competition

  • ✔ Candidates are allowed to participate as an Individual or as a Team (two students only).
  • ✔ Use of Canva or Microsoft Powerpoint are only allowed to make presentation.
  • ✔ Number of slides: 7-8 only.
  • ✔ Explanation of project ppt should not extend from 10 - 15 minutes of time limit.
