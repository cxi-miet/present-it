Topics for Competition

  • Mental Health
    The importance of mental health, stigma around it, and ways to improve it.
  • Cybersecurity
    The importance of protecting personal data, emerging threats, and preventive measures.
  • Gender Equality
    The fight for equal rights and opportunities for all genders, the impact of gender-based violence and discrimination.
  • Renewable Energy
    The growing importance of renewable energy sources like solar and wind power and their future prospects.
  • Social Media
    Its influence on society, the role of technology in shaping it, and the need for responsible use.
  • Immigration
    The impact of immigration policies on global economies, human rights concerns, and international relations.
  • Food Sustainabilit
    The challenges of global food production, food waste, and innovative solutions to ensure sustainable food production.
  • Space Exploration
    The future of space travel, potential benefits and challenges, and the role of private companies in space exploration.
  • Social Justice
    The fight for equal rights, justice, and equity for marginalized groups.
  • Cyberbullying
    Its impact on mental health, ways to prevent it, and legal and ethical considerations.

Instructions

Instructions for Competition

  • ✔ Candidates can participate as Individual or as a Team (2 students only).
  • ✔ Participants are required to develop a presentation on any of the given topics.
  • ✔ Selection will be done on the basis of submitted presentation.
  • ✔ Top 10 selected participants/teams will be explaining or presenting their ppt in front of the judges.
  • ✔ Cross questioning will take place: Judges will ask questions regarding to your presentation content.
  • ✔ Judging will be based on (70%) confidance & accuracy while ppt explaination and (30%) the content and presentation skills.

Rules

Rules for Competition

  • ✔ Candidates are allowed to participate as an Individual or as a Team (two students only).
  • ✔ Use of Canva or Microsoft Powerpoint are only allowed to make presentation.
  • ✔ Number of slides: 7-8 only.
  • ✔ Explanation of presentation should not extend from 10 - 15 minutes of time limit.

Rewards

Rewards of the Competition

  • ✔ 100% Certification to eligible candidates.
  • ✔ CASH PRIZE WORTH Rs.5000.
  • ✔ Enchancement in your communication skills and visual presentation skills.
